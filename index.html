Our Legacy

Healing Through Authentic Ayurveda

For over two decades, Srishti Clinic has been a trusted sanctuary of authentic Ayurvedic healing in Bhilai. Founded on the belief that true health comes from the balance of body, mind, and spirit.

Dr. Savita Golan, formerly an Ayurveda Medical Officer at Kawardha (Mohgaon), brings deep expertise in classical Ayurvedic treatments. Alongside Dr. Narendra Golan (MBBS), the clinic offers a rare fusion of traditional wisdom and modern medicine — under one roof.

"Swasthasya swasthya rakshanam, aturasya vikar prashamnam"— Preserve the health of the healthy; cure the disease of the diseased
